What is Culture Shocked?

About Us

Culture Shocked, is a site where users can find condensed, collated information on the history, culture, and society of different countries. The site is set to help ease the transition in travelling overseas and to help fend off the culture shock.

Resources

We give you insight into the environment, history, culture, and additional information to better prepare you for your travels. By creating an account, you can save pages of information to easily refer back to, as well as access our Community Discussion Board to broaden your knowledge even more.
